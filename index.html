Energized For STEM Student Council Applications




Council President

The council president will be the one to keep the student council in shape. The president will be the one who represents the council as whole, and to will orders from the faculty.

Requirements

  • The student council president is required to be a 12th grade student.
  • Closes September 2nd, 2022



Council Vice President

The Council Vice President's job is to delegate orders from the Council president and to take over if the president is not present for meetings or events. The student Vice President can be either an 11th or 12th grade student.

Requirements

  • The student council vice president is required to be a 12th grade student.
  • Closes September 2nd, 2022



Council Secretary

The council secretary will set up meeting schedules that the president has agreed on so that the student council can discuss important matters. They will also be the person who takes care of documents that are necessary for the student council.

Requirements

  • The student council secretary is required to be a 11th grade student.
  • The student council secretary is required to be a 12th grade student.



Council Treasurer

The council treasurer will organize events for the school to raise money for purchases highly requested by students. For example, Soccer goals or Volleyball Nets. The council treasurer will also speak with the faculty and see if school field trips or activities may be conducted with any raised funds.

Requirements

  • The student council treasurer should be a 12th grade student.
  • Preferably has above average mathematic grades.



Council Journalist

A Council Journalist would oversee “official” school accounts (such as an Instagram account under the school’s name) and will promote school spirit and activities through social media and other means. In addition, they will be the person who documents daily council activities and meetings as well.

Requirements

  • The student council journalist needs to be good with photography



Council Members

Consists of 4 students who qualify to be part of the student council. There will be 1 member from each grade respectively (9th,10th,11th,12th), if chosen, they will represent any thought that corresponds to the grade they represent. The council members will have a say in each decision of the student council and will file reports on their respective grade levels.

Requirements

  • Student can be in any grade level
